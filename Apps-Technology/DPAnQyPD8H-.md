### 3. Overall Message:

This video is a **guide and personal journey documenting the process of preparing for a trip to Japan by exchanging and digitalizing foreign currency.**

The core message revolves around:

1. **Practical Travel Preparation:** Exchanging physical currency (JPY and VND for transit).
2. **Currency Value and Exchange Rates:** Showing the current low rate of the Yen (104) and providing surprising comparisons of the value of JPY and VND notes in Indonesian Rupiah.
3. **Tips for Overseas Transactions:** Educating viewers that modern international debit cards (specifically those with contactless features and multi-currency accounts like CIMB Niaga's OCTO) can be used for transactions in Japan, offering an alternative to physical cash.
4. **Demonstrating Digital Foreign Exchange:** Showing how to purchase and load Japanese Yen onto a digital foreign currency account using a mobile banking app.
