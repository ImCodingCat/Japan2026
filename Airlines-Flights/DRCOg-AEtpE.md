### Audio/Speech Summary:
The speaker, at an airport check-in counter, informs viewers (those intending to fly) what they need to prepare when approaching the check-in counter. The essential items are the flight booking confirmation and official identification. The speaker specifies that acceptable forms of identification include a national ID card (KTP), a driver's license (SIM), or even a passport, even if it's a domestic flight. It is stressed that travelers must have at least one of these official forms of ID ready. The check-in personnel will verify the flight booking and ensure the identity presented (ID/driver's license/passport) matches the person flying, often by comparing the document to the traveler's face.

### Overall Message:
The video's core message is a reminder and guide for preparing the necessary identification and flight booking documents for checking in at the airport for any flight, emphasizing that various official identification cards (KTP, SIM, Passport) are acceptable, even for domestic travel, and are required to verify the traveler's identity.
