*   **Phrase 1 (Konnichiwa):** When entering, staff will greet you with "Irasshaimase!" (Welcome!). You don't need to say much; just smile and say "Konnichiwa."
*   **Phrase 2 (Sumimasen):** To call a staff member for help, it’s okay to raise your hand and say "Sumimasen" (Excuse me).
*   **Phrase 3 (Onegaishimasu / Kore onegaishimasu):** To order, point to the menu and say "[Item name] onegaishimasu" or "Kore onegaishimasu" (Can I have this?). The item placeholder ($\bigcirc$$\bigcirc$) can be replaced with anything like ramen, sushi, and beer.
*   **Phrase 4 (Ijou de!):** To say "that’s all" after finishing your order, say "Ijou de!"
*   **Phrase 5 (Okaikei, onegaishimasu):** When you are done eating, say this to ask for the check: "Okaikei, onegaishimasu."
*   **Phrase 6 (Gochisousamadeshita):** This phrase means "thanks for the meal" and is used when leaving. Locals really use it, and using it will impress everyone.
